# Audio Encoding

Most common audio encoding formats:

| Standard | Name |
| --- | --- |
| ISO/IEC 11172 | MPEG-1 Audio (MP3 or MPEG-1 part 3) |
| ISO/IEC 13818-3 | MPEG-2 Audio (MPEG-2 part 3) |
| ISO/IEC 13818-7 | AAC (MPEG-2 Part 7) |
| ISO/IEC 14496-3 | MPEG-4 Audio (MPEG-4 part 3) |
